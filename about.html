About Hero Healing Directory

Our Founder

 Hero Healing Directory was founded by John Anderson, a decorated veteran who served 20 years in the United States Army. John began his military career as an infantryman, rising through the ranks to become a Senior Non-Commissioned Officer (NCO). He served multiple tours in Iraq and Afghanistan, where he received the Bronze Star for his bravery and leadership during combat operations.

Our Mission

 The mission of Hero Healing Directory is to connect veterans and military personnel with the organizations and services they need to thrive. We believe that every veteran deserves support, recognition, and opportunities to succeed in civilian life. Our directory includes a wide range of resources, from financial assistance and employment services to mental health support and community engagement programs.

Our Vision

 We are committed to continuously updating and expanding our directory to ensure that veterans have access to the best resources available. Our goal is to empower veterans to overcome challenges and achieve their full potential. Thank you for visiting Hero Healing Directory, and we hope our resources can help you or someone you know.
